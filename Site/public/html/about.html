MIST (Mathematical Image Synthesizing Tool) isn't just about the pretty pictures. By using the MISt application, you will explore functional problem solving and computational thinking by creating non-representational images. Computational thinking is an essential part of computer science, and MIST allows you to devleop this skill. Anyone can use MIST because it does not require any prior experience in computer science. YOU have the option of creating images from scratch, or completing tutorials and challenges that encourage problem solving and computational thinking through image building. MIST also encourages interactions between users. YOu can create an account to see, share, and modify the work of other uses. Our goal is to develop a community where users enjoy learning computational thinking by associating mathematical functions with interesting images.
